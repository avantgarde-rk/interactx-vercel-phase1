About Us

At InteractX, we believe that every child deserves a supportive and engaging learning environment.

Our mission is to create a safe, interactive, and inclusive virtual world where children can develop essential life skills, improve social interactions, and build confidence through experiential learning. By combining cutting-edge VR technology with evidence-based educational practices, we design tailored modules that make learning fun, effective, and impactful.

Our Vision:

To empower children with special needs by providing a virtual learning platform that fosters growth, independence, and self-confidence.

Our Mission:

To bridge the gap between traditional learning and modern technology by delivering immersive experiences that nurture cognitive, social, and emotional development.
